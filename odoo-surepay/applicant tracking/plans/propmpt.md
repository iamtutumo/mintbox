ERP Onboarding Journey Requirements
1. Application & Candidate Intake
•	Online Application Portal
o	Candidates apply via a web form integrated into the ERP.
o	Required fields: Full Name, Email, Phone Number, Cover Letter(Create a field to be typed in), Position Applied For(drop down menu from available positions), Education(Can add different qualifications), Work Experience(Applicant can add different work experiences. Fields to include are : Start date | End date | Position | Responsibilities | Achievements), Skills, Referees(Candidate should input Referees name, Contact, Email, Place of work, etc.
o	Data should automatically populate into the HR module.
•	Eligibility Screening (Automated Filter)
o	Require HR user to enter the job requirements: (Number of years of work experience 0-2yrs, 3-5yrs, 6-10yrs, More than 10 yrs| Education level | Field of study | 
o	System compares applicant data against predefined job requirements (e.g., years of experience, education level, certifications, skills).
o	Applicants failing to meet minimum requirements are automatically flagged as Unsuccessful.
o	Automated Email Notification is triggered to inform them of rejection.
2. Application Tracking & Workflow
•	Applicant Tracking Stages:
1.	Application Received
2.	Eligibility Screening
3.	Shortlisting
4.	Recruitment committee review
5.	Interview stage
6.	Recruitment committee
7.	Offer Stage
8.	Onboarding
•	Dashboard View
1.	HR, Department Head, and CEO/Managing Director can track candidates through the pipeline.
2.	Visual indicators (stage tags) for each applicant.
3.	Advanced filtering and search (by position, department, application status, etc.).
•	Role-Based Access:
1.	Head of Department: Can review applicants for their department, add evaluation notes, approve/reject for shortlisting.
2.	HR Head: Can review all applicants, manage the recruitment pipeline, approve/reject, initiate communications.
3.	CEO/Managing Director: Has final visibility and approval rights for shortlisted candidates.

3. Automated Communications
•	Unsuccessful at Eligibility Screening (Template 1):

We truly appreciate the time and effort you invested in your application and encourage you to apply for future opportunities with us that better match your qualifications.
We wish you every success in your career journey.
Best regards,
SurePay Ltd HR Team
•	Unsuccessful After Shortlisting (Template 2):
Subject: Application Outcome – [Position Title]
Dear [First Name],
Thank you for your interest in the [Position Title] role at SurePay Ltd. We greatly appreciate the time you took to apply and go through the selection process.

We encourage you to stay connected with us and explore other opportunities that may be a better fit in the future.

Best regards,
SurePay Ltd HR Team
4. Shortlisting & Interview Process
•	System Shortlisting Tools:
o	Allows multiple reviewers (Department Head, HR, CEO) to score or rate candidates.
o	Supports attachments of interview notes, test results, or panel feedback.
5. Final Selection & Onboarding
•	Onboarding Portal:
o	Once a candidate accepts an offer, they gain access to an onboarding dashboard.
o	Features include:
	Uploading personal documents (ID, Tax PIN, NSSF/Insurance details, bank details).
	Digital signing of employment contract.
	Access to employee handbook and company policies.
	Task checklist (orientation sessions, training modules, equipment request, buddy assignment).
•	Employee Profile Creation:
o	Accepted candidate’s data is automatically transferred into the HR module as staff member
o	Generates employee ID, assigns department, reporting manager, payroll setup.
6. Additional Enhancements (Best Practices from Modern ERPs)
•	Analytics & Reporting:
o	Track recruitment metrics (e.g., time-to-hire, source of applicants, success rate at each stage).
o	Exportable reports for HR.
•	Compliance & Audit Trail:
o	Full history of each applicant’s journey (applied date, actions taken, reviewers’ comments).
•	Candidate Experience:
o	Self-service portal where candidates can check their application status.
o	Notifications for each stage (application received, shortlisted, interview scheduled, outcome).
